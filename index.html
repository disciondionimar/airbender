A1 Take On Me

"Take On Me" is a song by Norwegian synth-pop band A-ha, first released in 1984. The original version was produced by Tony Mansfield and remixed by John Ratcliff. A new version was released in 1985 and produced by Alan Tarney for the group's debut studio album Hunting High and Low. The song combines synthpop with a varied instrumentation that includes acoustic guitars, keyboards, and drums.
